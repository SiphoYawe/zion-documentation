Glossary of terms used across ACE (Accelerated Christian Education) methodology and the Zion app. Terms are grouped by area. Each entry links to the page where the feature is covered in full.

## ACE Methodology Terms

### PACE

**Definition:**
Packet of Accelerated Christian Education. A self-contained workbook covering one topic or unit within a subject, designed for self-paced, individualized learning.

**Details:**
- The PACE catalog spans numbers 1001 to 1756, across 23 subjects and levels 1 to 12.
- Each student works through PACEs in sequence within a subject. A student cannot be assigned PACE 1015 until every earlier PACE below it in that subject has been passed, unless an administrator records a progression override with an intervention plan.
- The catalog itself is a read-only reference: searchable and filterable by subject and level, not editable.

**See Also:** [PACE Catalog](/features/paces/catalog), [PACE Assignments](/features/students/pace-assignments)

---

### Goal Check

**Definition:**
The daily accountability system where students set PACE page goals each morning and staff mark actual progress at the end of the day.

**Components:**
1. Morning: set the goal (a target end page for each active PACE; the start page is derived automatically from where the student last left off).
2. Through the day: the student works toward the goal.
3. End of day: staff mark the actual end page reached.
4. Zion computes the variance between actual and goal.
5. Reports summarize the day or week.

**Example:**
Goal: pages 15 to 20 (6 pages). Actual: pages 15 to 18 (4 pages). Variance: -2 pages, a missed goal.

**See Also:** [Goal Check Overview](/features/goal-check/overview)

---

### The Ritual

**Definition:**
A full-screen, one-student-at-a-time guided flow that runs Goal Check for an entire learning centre in a single session. There are two rituals: the Morning Ritual (setting goals) and the End-of-Day Check (marking results). Both write to the same Goal Check data as the standalone entry screens, so there is no separate ritual data store.

**How it works:**
- Starting a ritual creates a session and queues every active student in the centre.
- Staff step through students one at a time, entering a goal end page (AM) or a Hit, Missed, or Partial result with actual pages (PM).
- A session can be paused and resumed later, and staff can go back, skip a student, jump to any student from a roster list, or skip all remaining students to finish early.
- Some schools require a supervisor or administrator to approve a session&apos;s morning goals before they are considered final. A monitor cannot approve.

**See Also:** [The Ritual](/features/goal-check/rituals)

---

### Learning Centre

**Definition:**
A classroom or learning space where students work on PACEs under staff supervision. It is also the scoping unit in Zion: every student belongs to one learning centre, and a supervisor or monitor sees only the students in the centres they are assigned to.

**In Zion:**
- Determines which staff member sees which students across Goal Check, attendance, grading, behaviour, and homework.
- Administrators see every centre in the organization. Supervisors and monitors are restricted server-side to their assigned centres, even if a client-side filter requests a broader view.
- Used to filter dashboards, reports, and exports.

**See Also:** [Learning Centres](/features/organization/learning-centres)

---

### Supervisor

**Definition:**
Staff member responsible for one or more learning centres: running the daily ritual, monitoring student progress, entering grades and behaviour records, and generating centre reports.

**In Zion:**
- Scoped to assigned learning centres only.
- Can set goals, mark progress, log behaviour, enter and moderate grades (if delegated), and generate reports for those centres.

**See Also:** [Supervisor Guide](/guides/supervisor/goal-check), [Roles &amp; Permissions](/reference/roles-permissions)

---

### Monitor

**Definition:**
Staff member who supports the daily ritual and other centre-level data entry (goals, progress, homework verification, behaviour logging) within their assigned learning centres.

**In Zion:**
- Scoped to assigned learning centres only, the same as a supervisor.
- Cannot approve morning-goal sign-off sessions or perform administrator-only actions such as deleting an academic term.

**See Also:** [Monitor Guide](/guides/monitor/goal-check), [Roles &amp; Permissions](/reference/roles-permissions)

---

### Variance

**Definition:**
The difference between actual pages completed and goal pages set for a student on a given day.

**Formula:**
```
Variance = Actual Total Pages - Goal Total Pages
```

**Interpretation:**
- Positive: the student exceeded the goal.
- Zero: the student met the goal exactly.
- Negative: the student missed the goal.

**See Also:** [Marking Progress](/features/goal-check/marking-progress)

---

### Completion Rate

**Definition:**
The percentage of students who met or exceeded their daily goal.

**Formula:**
```
Completion Rate = (Students Completed + Students Exceeded) / Total Students Marked x 100
```

**See Also:** [Supervisor Dashboard](/guides/supervisor/dashboard)

## Grading and Moderation Terms

### Test Type

**Definition:**
Every score entered in Zion has a test type: `pace_test`, `final_test`, or `self_test`. A `self_test` is an ungraded readiness check and always finalizes immediately. A `pace_test` or `final_test` is a graded score and may route through moderation.

**See Also:** [Grading Overview](/features/grading/overview), [Score Entry](/features/grading/score-entry)

---

### Moderation Floor

**Definition:**
The grade level at and above which a graded score enters the moderation pipeline instead of finalizing immediately. The default floor is Grade 8, and it is configurable per school.

**See Also:** [Moderation](/features/grading/moderation)

---

### Moderation Stages

**Definition:**
The stages a graded score moves through under moderation. A `self_test` never enters this pipeline.

| Stage | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Stage 1 pending | Entered by staff, awaiting internal moderation. |
| Stage 1 rejected | Sent back to the original grader for resubmission, with a mandatory reason. |
| Stage 2 internal approved | Approved by an internal moderator, awaiting external moderation. |
| Stage 3 external approved | Finalized. The score is locked and cannot be changed outside the audited correction path. |
| Flagged for ACE review | Escalated to accreditation-body review instead of finalizing. |

A moderator can never approve a score they entered themselves, and an internal moderator cannot also act as the external moderator on the same score.

**See Also:** [Moderation](/features/grading/moderation), [Audit Trail](/features/grading/audit-trail)

---

## Attendance and Behaviour Terms

### Behaviour Points

**Definition:**
A signed point value attached to a behaviour record. Every record is one of three types, and the sign of its points is enforced by type: merit points are zero or positive, demerit points are zero or negative, incident points can be either.

**In Zion:**
- Categories (with a default point value per category) are configured by an administrator and can be archived, never hard-deleted, so historical records stay intact.
- A student&apos;s net balance is the signed sum of every record logged against them, not just what is visible on screen.
- A public note on a record is visible to parents. A private staff note is never shown to parents.

**See Also:** [Behaviour Overview](/features/behaviour/overview), [Logging Behaviour](/features/behaviour/logging)

---

## Homework Terms

### Homework Verification

**Definition:**
The step where a teacher or staff member confirms that a homework assignment is done. Verification is a status change, not a grade or feedback entry.

**Statuses:**
- Pending: not yet marked done by the family or verified by staff.
- Parent done: the family marked the assignment done; awaiting teacher confirmation.
- Teacher verified: confirmed done, whether the family marked it first or staff marked it directly.
- Missed: past due and never verified. A missed assignment can still be verified late.

**See Also:** [Assigning and Verifying Homework](/features/homework/assigning-and-verifying)

## PACE, Roster, and Records Terms

### Centre Scoping

**Definition:**
The access rule that limits a supervisor or monitor to the learning centres they are assigned to, enforced on the server rather than only in the interface. An administrator is not scoped and sees the whole organization.

**Where it applies:**
Student rosters, Goal Check, grading and moderation delegation, behaviour records, homework, attendance, and reports all apply centre scoping to non-administrator staff.

**See Also:** [Learning Centres](/features/organization/learning-centres), [Roles &amp; Permissions](/reference/roles-permissions)

---

### Permanent Record

**Definition:**
The multi-year cumulative academic record for a student: percentage, PACEs completed, and attendance, broken down by year and subject. It does not include credits, GPA, ranking, or honor roll.

**Who can view it:**
Administrators, supervisors, and monitors can view any in-org student&apos;s permanent record. Only an administrator can manage it. A parent can view their own linked child&apos;s record, read-only.

**See Also:** [Permanent Record](/features/report-cards/permanent-record)

---

### Entitlement

**Definition:**
Whether an organization&apos;s subscription is currently active and in good standing. Zion checks entitlement before write actions across the app; an organization that is not entitled is routed to a subscription-required screen rather than allowed to proceed.

**See Also:** [Billing Overview](/features/billing/overview)

---

### Academic Term

**Definition:**
A school term configured in Zion for tracking Goal Check, grading, and reporting within a defined date range. Exactly one term per organization can be marked current at a time; setting a new term as current clears the flag from every other term.

**See Also:** [Academic Terms](/features/academic-terms/overview)

---

## Status and States

### Student Status

**Active:**
- The student is currently enrolled, appears in Goal Check, dashboards, and reports, and is counted in all metrics.

**Deactivated:**
- The student has withdrawn, transferred, or graduated. Hidden from active lists by default. Cannot have new goals set. Historical data is preserved.

**See Also:** [Student Roster](/features/students/overview)

---

### Goal Check Status

**Not Started:**
- Progress has not been marked for the day.

**Completed:**
- Actual pages met or slightly exceeded the goal.

**Exceeded:**
- Actual pages significantly exceeded the goal.

**Missed:**
- Actual pages fell short of the goal.

**See Also:** [Marking Progress](/features/goal-check/marking-progress)

---

## Acronyms

**ACE**: Accelerated Christian Education, the educational methodology and curriculum provider Zion is built for.

**PACE**: Packet of Accelerated Christian Education.

**CSV**: Comma-Separated Values, the file format used for bulk import and export.

**KPI**: Key Performance Indicator, a summary metric shown on dashboards.

**DOB**: Date of Birth.

**RLS**: Row-Level Security, the Supabase database access-control model Zion relies on for centre and organization scoping.
