# Supervisor Attendance Guide

As a supervisor, you&apos;re responsible for marking daily attendance for students in your assigned learning centre(s). This guide covers the complete daily workflow for attendance management.

## Your Attendance Responsibilities

### Daily (5-10 minutes)

- Mark attendance for all students by end of day
- Record late arrivals with arrival times
- Add notes for known absences
- Review your attendance dashboard

### Weekly

- Review weekly attendance for your centre
- Check for patterns (students frequently absent)
- Report concerns to administrator

---

## Daily Marking Workflow

### Step 1: Access Attendance

1. Log into Zion
2. Navigate to **Attendance** in sidebar
3. The marking interface opens

### Step 2: Verify Date and Centre

- **Date:** Defaults to today (change if marking previous day)
- **Centre:** Select your learning centre from dropdown

<Note>
**Your Centres Only:** You can only see and mark attendance for students in your assigned learning centre(s). If a student doesn&apos;t appear, verify they&apos;re assigned to your centre.
</Note>

### Step 3: Mark Each Student

For each student in the list:

**Present (Green Button):**
- Student is in class on time
- Click once to mark
- Status saves immediately

**Absent (Red Button):**
- Student is not present
- Click once to mark
- Consider adding a note if you know the reason

**Late (Amber Button):**
- Student arrived after school start
- Click to mark
- Optionally set arrival time
- Add note about reason if known

### Step 4: Use Bulk Actions

For efficiency when most students are present:

1. Click **Mark All Present** button
2. Confirm in dialog
3. Then individually mark absent/late students

### Step 5: Add Notes

For any student, add notes:

1. Click the notes icon on student row
2. Enter explanation (e.g., "Doctor appointment")
3. Notes are visible to administrators

### Step 6: Verify Completion

Check the summary bar:
- **Total:** All students in your centre
- **Marked:** Students with status
- **Unmarked:** Students still needing status

All students should be marked before you leave for the day.

---

## The Marking Interface

### Student List

Students are sorted for efficiency:
1. **Unmarked students first** (no status yet)
2. **Then alphabetically** by first name

Each row shows:
- Student name
- Current status (or "Unmarked")
- Notes indicator (if notes exist)
- Grade level

### Status Buttons

| Button | Color | Icon | Meaning |
|--------|-------|------|---------|
| Present | Green | Checkmark | On time |
| Absent | Red | X | Not present |
| Late | Amber | Clock | Arrived late |

### Summary Bar

Real-time totals update as you mark:
- Total students
- Marked count
- Unmarked count
- Present/Absent/Late breakdown

## Handling Special Cases

### Late Arrivals

When a student arrives late:

1. Mark status as **Late** (amber)
2. Click time icon to set arrival time
3. Optionally add note with reason

**Recording Time:**
- Use the time picker
- Set actual arrival time
- This helps track patterns

### Known Absences

If you know why a student is absent:

1. Mark status as **Absent** (red)
2. Click notes icon
3. Enter reason: "Doctor appointment", "Family trip", etc.

**Common Notes:**
- "Parent called - sick"
- "Dentist appointment"
- "Family emergency"
- "School-approved absence"

### Correcting Mistakes

If you mark wrong status:

1. Click the correct status button
2. New status replaces old
3. Changes save immediately

### Previous Day Marking

To mark attendance for yesterday:

1. Click the date picker
2. Select previous date
3. Mark as normal

<Warning>
**Midnight Cutoff:** You can only mark attendance for a date before midnight of that date. After midnight, contact your administrator for corrections.
</Warning>

## Your Attendance Dashboard

### Accessing Dashboard

1. Navigate to **Attendance → Dashboard**
2. View your centre&apos;s attendance metrics

### What You See

**Today&apos;s Summary:**
- Your centre&apos;s attendance rate
- Present/Absent/Late counts
- Comparison to school average

**Absent Students List:**
- Students absent today
- Quick reference for follow-up

**Late Arrivals:**
- Students who arrived late
- Arrival times

**Weekly Trend:**
- Last 7 days for your centre
- Visual trend line

## Viewing Alerts

### Your Centre&apos;s Alerts

1. Navigate to **Attendance → Alerts**
2. See alerts for your students only

### Alert Information

Each alert shows:
- Student name
- Alert type (Warning/Critical)
- Consecutive absences
- 30-day absence rate

### What to Do

When you see an alert for your student:

1. Note the pattern
2. Talk to the student if possible
3. Inform administrator
4. Document any actions in notes

<Tip>
**You Cannot Acknowledge:** Only administrators can acknowledge alerts. Your role is to be aware and report concerns to administration.
</Tip>

## Best Practices

### Timing

**Morning Mark (Recommended):**
- Mark attendance within first 15 minutes
- Most accurate for on-time vs late

**Mid-Morning Update:**
- Update any late arrivals with times
- Add notes for known reasons

**End of Day Verify:**
- Ensure all students marked
- Check summary shows zero unmarked

### Accuracy

- **Mark in real-time:** Don&apos;t batch mark at end of day
- **Be precise:** Late vs Absent matters for tracking
- **Use notes:** Document known reasons
- **Double-check:** Verify before leaving

### Consistency

- Mark at same time each day
- Use consistent note terminology
- Follow same process daily
- Don&apos;t skip days

## Common Issues

### Student Not Appearing

**Possible Causes:**
- Student not assigned to your centre
- Student deactivated
- Wrong centre selected

**Solution:**
- Verify centre selection
- Contact administrator if student should be there

### Cannot Mark Past Date

**Cause:** Midnight cutoff has passed

**Solution:**
- Contact administrator for correction
- Mark current day on time going forward

### Status Not Saving

**Possible Causes:**
- Network issue
- Session expired

**Solution:**
- Check internet connection
- Refresh page
- Log out and back in

## Exporting Your Data

### CSV Export

Export your centre&apos;s attendance:

1. Navigate to **Attendance** marking page
2. Click **Export** button
3. CSV downloads automatically

**File Contains:**
- Student name and number
- Grade level
- Status (Present/Absent/Late)
- Arrival time (if late)
- Notes
- Summary totals

### Use Cases

- Parent meeting preparation
- Personal records
- Spot-checking data
