Zion is a school management app for ACE (Accelerated Christian Education) schools. It covers the daily Goal Check ritual, attendance, grading and moderation, behaviour tracking, homework, extracurriculars, the library, PACE inventory, parent-teacher meetings, the school calendar, broadcasts and notifications, report cards, admissions, analytics, billing, and dedicated portals for parents and students.

## Who uses Zion

Zion has five roles. Each one reaches a different slice of the app.

| Role | What they do in Zion |
|------|----------------------|
| Administrator | Full access to all school data and settings; holds the organization account and billing |
| Supervisor | Centre-level access to assigned learning centres |
| Monitor | Data entry for assigned centres: goals, attendance, and scores |
| Parent | Views their own child&apos;s data |
| Student | Views their own data |

See <a href="/reference/roles-permissions">Roles and permissions</a> for the full permission matrix.
