# Welcome to Zion

Zion is an AI-powered school management system designed specifically for **ACE (Accelerated Christian Education) schools**. We transform time-consuming manual processes into intelligent automation, liberating administrators and supervisors to focus on what matters most: **student success**.

<Card title="The Magic" icon="sparkles" color="blue">
  Transform 2-3 hours of daily Goal Check compilation into less than 1 minute of automated reporting
</Card>

## What is Zion?

Zion automates the core administrative workflows unique to ACE schools:

- **Goal Check Intelligence** - Daily student goal tracking with real-time dashboards and one-click reporting
- **Student Management** - Individualized PACE curriculum tracking for 144 PACEs across 12 subjects
- **Learning Centre Administration** - Multi-centre school management with supervisor assignments
- **Analytics & Insights** - Trend analysis, predictive alerts, and data-driven decision making

## Who is Zion For?

### School Administrators
Get instant school-wide intelligence through real-time dashboards, generate weekly reports in seconds, and make data-driven decisions with confidence.

### Supervisors
Monitor your learning centres in real-time, identify struggling students early, and generate daily centre reports when all students are marked.

### Monitors
Set daily goals and mark student progress efficiently with tablet-optimized interfaces designed for on-the-go data entry.
