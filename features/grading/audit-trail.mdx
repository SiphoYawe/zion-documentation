Zion writes a record to the grading audit log for every score entry, adjustment, moderation decision, retest action, override, and reassignment. Administrators use the Audit Log page to investigate a specific score, review moderation compliance, and export evidence for ACE accreditation.

<Note>
  The audit log is administrator-facing. Supervisors and monitors see the moderation queues and alerts covered in [Moderation queue](/features/grading/moderation), but the full Audit Log page (`/admin/grading/audit`) is scoped to administrators.
</Note>

## What gets logged

Every grading mutation writes a row with an action type constrained to a fixed set:

| Action type | Written when |
|---|---|
| `score_entered` | A test score is entered at [score entry](/features/grading/score-entry) |
| `score_adjusted` | A moderator changes a score value, or a rejected score is resubmitted |
| `moderation_approved` | A score passes internal or external moderation |
| `moderation_rejected` | A moderator rejects a score back to Stage 1 with a reason |
| `grade_finalized` | External moderation approves and locks a score |
| `retest_scheduled` | A retest is scheduled for a failed test |
| `retest_completed` | A retest score is entered |
| `admin_override` | An administrator grants a retest cap override or approves progression |
| `moderation_reassigned` | An administrator reassigns a stuck moderation to another staff member |

Each entry records the student, PACE, the user who performed the action and their role at the time, and the timestamp. Adjustments and rejections also record the previous value, the new value, and the reason given, since a reason of at least 10 characters is required for both.

## Viewing the audit log

<Frame caption="The grading audit log with filters and a score history timeline.">
  <img src="/images/features/grading/audit-trail-1.png" alt="Grading audit log listing score entries and moderation actions" />
</Frame>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Audit Log">
    Navigate to Grading &gt; Audit Log (`/admin/grading/audit`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter">
    Filter by date range, action type, learning centre, performed-by user, or student.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review a score's history">
    Open a single score to see its full history timeline, from entry through every moderation step to finalization.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export">
    Export the filtered log as CSV, or generate one of the compliance reports below.
  </Step>
</Steps>

This is the same page you would use to trace a parent-reported score discrepancy: pull the score&apos;s history, compare the original entry to the current value, and identify who changed it and why.

## Compliance reports

From the Audit Log page, administrators can generate four report types:

| Report | Covers |
|---|---|
| All Grade Changes | Every logged action across the filtered range |
| Moderation Compliance | Approvals, rejections, and reassignments through the moderation pipeline |
| Score Adjustments | Every `score_adjusted` entry with its reason |
| Rejected Tests | Every `moderation_rejected` entry and its resolution |

For an ACE inspection, administrators can also generate a one-click accreditation bundle: a ZIP export containing grades, moderation actions, the student roster, and term dates for the selected period.

## Audit integrity

The Audit Log page shows an audit-integrity verification badge reflecting the result of the latest scheduled tamper check: passed, pending, or tampered rows found. This is the check to look at before submitting an accreditation bundle, since it confirms the underlying log has not been altered outside the normal grading actions.
