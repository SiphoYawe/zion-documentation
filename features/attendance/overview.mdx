# Attendance Management Overview

Zion&apos;s Attendance module provides a complete digital solution for tracking student presence, managing chronic absence alerts, generating reports, and analyzing attendance trends across your school.

## What is Attendance Management?

In ACE schools, daily attendance tracking is essential for student accountability and regulatory compliance. Zion transforms this process from paper-based record-keeping into a streamlined digital workflow with intelligent alerting.

**Traditional Attendance (Manual):**
- Paper roll books or spreadsheets
- Manual compilation for reports
- No chronic absence detection
- Difficult to identify patterns
- Time required: 30+ minutes daily

**Zion Attendance (Digital):**
- Touch-optimized digital marking
- One-tap status updates (Present/Absent/Late)
- Automatic chronic absence alerts
- Trend analysis and pattern detection
- CSV/PDF export for compliance
- Time required: &lt;5 minutes daily

**Time Saved:** 30+ minutes → 5 minutes = **25+ minutes saved daily**

---

## How Attendance Works in Zion

### Morning: Attendance Marking

**Who:** Supervisors or Administrators
**Where:** Attendance → Mark Attendance
**Time:** 3-5 minutes for 30 students

1. Select today&apos;s date (defaults to current date)
2. Select learning centre (optional for admins, required for supervisors)
3. For each student, tap status:
   - **Present** (green) - Student is in class
   - **Absent** (red) - Student is not present
   - **Late** (amber) - Student arrived after start time
4. For late arrivals, optionally record arrival time
5. Add notes for any student (e.g., "doctor appointment")
6. Status auto-saves immediately

**Features:**
- Bulk "Mark All Present" for efficiency
- Tablet-optimized with large 48x48px touch targets
- Real-time summary updates as you mark
- Students sorted: unmarked first, then alphabetically

---

### Throughout the Day: Dashboard Monitoring

**Who:** Administrators and Supervisors
**Where:** Attendance → Dashboard

**Dashboard Displays:**
- Today&apos;s attendance summary (Present/Absent/Late counts)
- Attendance rate percentage
- Absent students list with details
- Late arrivals list with arrival times
- Centre breakdown chart (admin view)
- Week-over-week comparison

**Real-Time Updates:**
- Dashboard refreshes automatically every 30 seconds
- See live progress as attendance is marked across centres

---

### Automated: Chronic Absence Alerts

**Who:** System-generated, reviewed by Administrators
**Where:** Attendance → Alerts
**When:** Daily at 6:00 AM (before school day)

**Alert Triggers:**

| Alert Type | Condition | Action Required |
|------------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Critical** | 5+ consecutive absences | Immediate parent contact |
| **Critical** | &gt;20% absence rate in 30 days | Parent meeting required |
| **Warning** | 3+ consecutive absences | Monitor closely |

**Alert Workflow:**
1. System analyzes previous 60 days of attendance
2. Counts consecutive absences (excluding weekends)
3. Calculates 30-day absence rate
4. Creates alerts for students meeting thresholds
5. Sends email digest to administrators (if enabled)
6. Alerts appear in dashboard for acknowledgment

## Key Features

### 1. 3-Tap Marking Workflow

Quick, intuitive attendance entry:

- **Tap 1:** Tap student row to expand
- **Tap 2:** Tap status button (Present/Absent/Late)
- **Tap 3:** Confirm or add optional notes

Each status shows visual confirmation:
- Green checkmark for Present
- Red X for Absent
- Amber clock for Late

### 2. Bulk Operations

For large classes or school-wide events:

- **Mark All Present:** One click marks all unmarked students as present
- **Clear All:** Reset all attendance for re-entry
- **Date Selection:** Mark attendance for previous days (before midnight cutoff)

### 3. Chronic Absence Detection

Proactive identification of attendance issues:

**Alert Generation:**
- Runs automatically each morning
- Analyzes rolling 60-day window
- Excludes weekends from consecutive counts
- Only creates new alerts if no unacknowledged alert exists

**Alert Management:**
- View all active alerts in dedicated panel
- Filter by type (Warning/Critical)
- Filter by learning centre
- Acknowledge alerts with intervention notes
- Track parent contacts and follow-up actions

### 4. Attendance Reports

**Report Types:**
- **Daily:** Single day attendance summary
- **Weekly:** 7-day overview with trends
- **Monthly:** Full month statistics
- **Term:** Complete term analysis
- **Custom:** User-specified date range

**Report Contents:**
- Overall attendance rate
- Centre-by-centre breakdown
- Chronic absence student list
- Late arrival patterns
- Day-of-week analysis
- Optional Goal Check correlation

### 5. Trend Analysis

**Trend Dashboard:**
- Line chart showing attendance over time
- Range options: 7 days, 30 days, term, custom
- Trend direction indicator (improving/stable/declining)
- Best and worst performing days identified
- Centre filtering for focused analysis

### 6. Export Capabilities

**CSV Export:**
- One-click download for any date
- Includes: Student name, number, grade, status, arrival time, notes
- Summary section with totals and attendance rate
- Filename: `attendance-{date}-{centre-name}.csv`

## Best Practices

### Daily Routine

1. **Morning (8:00 AM):** Mark attendance within first 15 minutes of school day
2. **Mid-Morning (10:00 AM):** Update any late arrivals with arrival times
3. **End of Day:** Review any missed students and complete marking

### Alert Management

- Review alerts daily (takes &lt;2 minutes)
- Acknowledge alerts after taking action (document intervention)
- Contact parents within 24 hours of critical alerts
- Use notes field to track conversations and follow-ups

### Accuracy Tips

- Mark attendance at consistent times each day
- Use notes for planned absences (field trips, appointments)
- Review previous day before moving to new day
- Export reports weekly for your records
