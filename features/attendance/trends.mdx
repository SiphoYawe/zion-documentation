Trends shows how attendance moves over time and how learning centres compare to each other. Administrators see it school-wide. Supervisors see it for the centres they are assigned to.

## Who can see this page

| Role | Access |
|------|--------|
| Administrator | All centres, with an option to filter to one centre |
| Supervisor | Their assigned centre or centres only |
| Monitor, teaching staff, parent, student | No access |

<Note>
Access is scoped the same way as the attendance dashboard: an administrator sees the full organization, a supervisor is limited to their assigned learning centres.
</Note>

## Viewing trends

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Attendance">
    From the sidebar, go to **Attendance**, then **Trends**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a range">
    Pick 7 days, 30 days, current term, or a custom start and end date.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter (optional)">
    Administrators can narrow the view to a single centre. You can also filter by grade level.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The trend chart

The chart plots a daily attendance rate across the selected range, using present, absent, and late counts recorded for each day.

Below the chart, Zion shows a summary:

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Average rate | Mean attendance rate across the selected range |
| Best day | Date with the highest attendance rate |
| Worst day | Date with the lowest attendance rate |
| Trend direction | Improving, stable, or declining |
| Trend change | Percent change associated with the trend direction |

## Centre comparison

Administrators can compare average attendance rate across centres in the same view. Centres are ordered by rate, so the strongest and weakest performers are easy to spot. Use this to check whether a dip is limited to one centre or affects the whole organization before deciding on an intervention.

A supervisor sees this data only for their own centre or centres, without a cross-organization comparison.

<Info>
Zion also surfaces a day-of-week breakdown alongside trends, showing how attendance rate varies by weekday within the selected range.
</Info>
