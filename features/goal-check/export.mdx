<Warning>
**This page is deprecated.** The dedicated Goal Check export at `/goal-check/export` no longer generates new exports. It shows a redirect notice pointing to the unified Data Export center, plus read-only access to its old export history.
</Warning>

## What changed

Goal Check data export moved into the unified Data Export center at `/settings/export`. From there, administrators and supervisors request export bundles covering multiple entities, including goal checks, in one job instead of a separate goal-check-only export.

If you land on `/goal-check/export`, the page tells you this and links to `/settings/export`.

## Where your old exports went

`/goal-check/export/history` still works for administrators during the deprecation window. It lists exports generated by the old page, read-only. Download links from that legacy history expire after 24 hours, which is shorter than the unified exporter&apos;s 7-day window.

<Note>
Any new goal check export you generate now, whether from the one-click &quot;Export everything&quot; button or a filtered request, goes through `/settings/export` and follows the unified 7-day download window instead.
</Note>

## How to export Goal Check data now

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    Open Settings, then select the Data Export tab (`/settings/export`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a date range">
    Pick a date range, or check &quot;All time&quot; to skip date picking and export full history.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Goal Checks as an entity">
    Choose goal_checks along with any other entities you need, such as students, attendance, or grades. Selecting goal_checks reveals optional filters for completion status and PACE subject.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scope the export (administrators only)">
    Administrators can scope to specific centres or a single student. Supervisors are automatically scoped to their assigned centre(s) and cannot change this.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit and download">
    The job queues, runs, and becomes ready. Download from the Export History table or from the emailed link, which stays valid for 7 days.
  </Step>
</Steps>
