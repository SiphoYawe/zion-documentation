This guide walks an administrator through the initial setup of a school in Zion: creating the account and organization, completing the onboarding wizard, adding students, assigning PACEs, and running the first Morning Ritual.

<Note>
This quickstart is for the administrator who creates the school&apos;s Zion organization. If you are a supervisor or monitor, an administrator invites you directly. See [Supervisor: Goal Check](/guides/supervisor/goal-check) or the [Rituals overview](/features/goal-check/rituals) for your daily workflow.
</Note>

## Prerequisites

Have ready before you start:
- School name, city, country, contact email and phone, and timezone
- A list of learning centres (classrooms) to create
- Names and email addresses for the supervisors and monitors you plan to invite
- Your student roster (name, date of birth, grade level, learning centre, optional parent contact), either to enter by hand or as a CSV file

## Step 1: Create your account and organization

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Create your administrator account with your email and password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    Click the verification link in the email from Clerk, Zion&apos;s authentication provider.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your organization">
    On first login, create your school&apos;s organization. This becomes the org that every learning centre, staff account, and student roster in Zion is scoped to.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Complete the onboarding wizard

After you create the organization, Zion redirects you to `/onboarding`, a forced, sequential wizard with four steps. Each step requires the previous one&apos;s data before you can continue, and it runs once per organization. If you return to `/onboarding` after finishing, Zion shows a completed screen with links to the dashboard and School Settings instead of the wizard.

### School profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter school details">
    School name (required), city, country, contact email, contact phone, and timezone. Language defaults to English.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Continue">
    Submit to move to Academic Terms.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Academic terms

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add a term">
    Enter a term name and a start and end date. You must create at least one term before you can continue.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add more terms (optional)">
    Add additional terms now, or return to [Academic Terms](/features/academic-terms/overview) later. Overlapping date ranges are rejected.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Learning centres

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add a centre">
    Enter a centre name, with an optional description and capacity. You must create at least one centre before you can continue.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add more centres">
    Repeat for each classroom or learning centre in your school. See [Learning Centres](/features/organization/learning-centres) for capacity and transfer details you can adjust later.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Invite teaching staff

<Steps>
  <Step title="Invite a supervisor or monitor">
    Enter a name and email, choose Supervisor or Monitor, and optionally assign the learning centres they work in. This step only offers the Supervisor and Monitor roles. Administrators are added separately from Settings, not through this wizard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Skip or send">
    Send the invitation, or click Skip This Step to invite staff later from [Teaching Staff and Users](/features/organization/teaching-staff-and-users).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finish">
    Zion shows a setup summary, then takes you to your administrator dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 3: Add students

With your terms and learning centres in place, add your student roster.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Add individually">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Students">
        Go to Students in the sidebar.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add Student">
        Click Add Student.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Fill in the student record">
        Enter full name, optional student ID, date of birth, grade level (1-12), learning centre, and optional parent email and phone.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Save">
        Save the record. If you entered a parent email, Zion sends a parent invitation automatically.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Bulk import via CSV">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Import CSV">
        On the Students page, click Import CSV, then download the template. See [CSV Templates](/reference/csv-templates) for the exact columns.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Fill in the file">
        Columns are name, student_id, dob, grade, centre, parent_email, and parent_phone. The centre value must match an existing learning centre name exactly. Files are capped at 5MB.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Validate">
        Upload the file and click Validate CSV. Zion checks each row and flags invalid rows without blocking the valid ones. A centre at or over its configured capacity produces a warning, not a block.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Import valid rows">
        Click Import to create the valid rows. Download the error report for any skipped rows, fix them, and re-upload if needed.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

See [Students Overview](/features/students/overview) and [Bulk Import](/features/students/bulk-import) for roster management after setup, including deactivating and reactivating students.

## Step 4: Assign PACEs

Students need at least one active PACE assignment before you can set a goal for them in the Ritual. Zion&apos;s PACE catalog covers the ACE curriculum across 23 subjects and levels 1 through 12.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a student">
    Click a student in the roster to open their detail page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign a PACE">
    Open the PACE assignment dialog and select one or more PACEs, up to 12 per assignment. Zion enforces subject progression: a student cannot be assigned a PACE until every earlier PACE in that subject has been passed, unless an administrator has recorded an override.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Save the assignment. It now appears on the student&apos;s profile and is available to goal in the Ritual.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Repeat">
    Repeat for each student. Students in the same grade often carry similar PACE assignments across subjects.
  </Step>
</Steps>

See [PACE Catalog](/features/paces/catalog) to browse subjects and levels, and [PACE Assignments](/features/students/pace-assignments) for progression rules, auto-advance, and completion.

## Step 5: Run your first Morning Ritual

The Morning Ritual is a full-screen, one-student-at-a-time flow for setting daily PACE goals across an entire learning centre in one pass.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the dashboard">
    Go to your dashboard and find the Today&apos;s Ritual card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a centre (if needed)">
    If you are assigned to more than one learning centre, select which one to run.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Morning Ritual">
    Click Start Morning Ritual. Zion opens the centre&apos;s active students one at a time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a goal for each student">
    For each active PACE, enter a goal end page. The start page is set automatically from where the student left off. Click Advance to move to the next student, or use Skip or Back as needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finish the session">
    After the last student, review the completion summary showing goals set, skipped students, and elapsed time. If your school requires goal approval, an administrator or supervisor can approve the session&apos;s pending goals from this screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
If you close the tab mid-session, reopening the Ritual resumes where you left off. Zion never starts a duplicate session for the same day.
</Tip>

At the end of the day, run the End-of-Day Check from the same Today&apos;s Ritual card to record actual pages completed against each goal. See [Rituals](/features/goal-check/rituals) for the full AM and PM workflow.
