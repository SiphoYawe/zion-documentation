# Quickstart Guide

Follow these steps to get started with Zion quickly.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

Visit [zionapp.co](https://zionapp.co) and sign up for a free account.

## Step 2: Set Up Your School Profile

1. Navigate to **Settings** > **School Profile**
2. Enter your school information
3. Upload your school logo (optional)
4. Click **Save**

## Step 3: Add Students

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Students">
    Click on **Students** in the sidebar navigation
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add New Student">Click the **Add Student** button</Step>
  <Step title="Enter Student Details">
    Fill in the student&apos;s name, grade level, and other information
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">Click **Save** to add the student to your system</Step>
</Steps>

## Step 4: Create Reading Goals

1. Select a student from your student list
2. Click **Set Goal**
3. Configure the goal parameters (pages per day, duration, etc.)
4. Click **Create Goal**
