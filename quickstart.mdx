# Quickstart Guide

Get your ACE school operational in Zion in just 15 minutes. This guide walks you through the complete setup process from account creation to your first Goal Check.

<Note>
**New to Zion?** This quickstart is designed for school administrators setting up Zion for the first time. If you&apos;re a supervisor or monitor, your administrator will invite you - skip to [Daily Goal Check Workflow](/guides/supervisor/goal-check).
</Note>

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, have ready:
- School information (name, location, contact details)
- School logo (optional, PNG/JPG, max 2MB)
- List of learning centres (classrooms)
- List of teaching staff (names and emails for supervisors/monitors)
- Student roster (names, grades, learning centre assignments)

<Tip>
**Pro Tip:** Have your student data in a CSV file? You can bulk import students later to save time.
</Tip>

## Step 1: Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Visit [zionapp.co/sign-up](https://zionapp.co/sign-up) and create your administrator account with your email and password.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Email">
    Check your inbox for the verification email from Clerk and click the verification link.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Organization">
    Upon first login, you&apos;ll be prompted to create your school organization. Enter your school name and submit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Time Required:** 2 minutes

---

## Step 2: Complete School Onboarding

Zion&apos;s guided onboarding wizard will walk you through four essential setup steps. You&apos;ll be automatically redirected to `/onboarding` after creating your organization.

### School Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter School Details">
    - School name (auto-filled from organization)
    - Location (city and country)
    - Contact email and phone number
    - Time zone (e.g., "Africa/Kampala")
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload Logo (Optional)">
    Click "Upload Logo" and select your school logo. This will appear in reports and navigation.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save and Continue">
    Click "Save & Continue" to proceed to academic terms.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Academic Terms

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create First Term">
    Click "Add Term" and enter:
    - Term name (e.g., "Term 1 2025")
    - Start date (e.g., October 1, 2025)
    - End date (e.g., December 20, 2025)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Mark as Active">
    Check "Set as active term" to indicate this is your current term. Goal Check data will be tracked within this term.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add More Terms (Optional)">
    You can add all your terms now or add them later. Click "Save & Continue" when ready.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Learning Centres

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Learning Centres">
    Add each classroom/learning centre:
    - Centre name (e.g., "Faith Centre", "Grace Centre", "Hope Centre")
    - Description (optional)
    - Capacity (optional, e.g., 25 students)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Multiple Centres">
    Click "Add Another Centre" to create additional centres. Most ACE schools have 3-10 learning centres.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save and Continue">
    Click "Save & Continue" when all centres are added.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Invite Teaching Staff

<Steps>
  <Step title="Invite Supervisors">
    For each supervisor:
    - Full name
    - Email address
    - Role: Select "Supervisor"
    - Assigned centres: Select which learning centres they supervise
  </Step>

  <Step title="Invite Monitors">
    For each monitor:
    - Full name
    - Email address
    - Role: Select "Monitor"
    - Assigned centres: Select which centres they assist in
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send Invitations">
    Click "Send Invitations" and staff will receive email invitations with instructions to set their passwords and access Zion.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Onboarding">
    Click "Complete Setup" to finish onboarding. You&apos;ll be redirected to your administrator dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Time Required:** 10 minutes

---

## Step 3: Add Students

Now that your school structure is ready, add your student roster.

### Option A: Add Students Individually

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Students">
    Click "Students" in the sidebar navigation.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Add Student">
    Click the "Add Student" button in the top right.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill Student Form">
    Enter student information:
    - Full name
    - Student ID (optional, your internal ID)
    - Date of birth
    - Grade level (1-12)
    - Assigned learning centre
    - Parent email and phone (optional)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Student">
    Click "Save" to add the student to your roster.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Option B: Bulk Import via CSV (Recommended for 20+ students)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download CSV Template">
    On the Students page, click "Import CSV" and then "Download Template" to get the correct CSV format.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill CSV File">
    Open the template in Excel and fill in student data with columns:
    - name (required)
    - student_id (optional)
    - dob (format: YYYY-MM-DD)
    - grade (1-12)
    - centre (exact learning centre name)
    - parent_email (optional)
    - parent_phone (optional)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload CSV">
    Click "Import CSV", select your file, and click "Upload". Zion will validate your data and show any errors.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Import">
    Review the validation results. If all rows are valid, click "Confirm Import" to create all students at once.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Time Required:** 3-5 minutes (individual) or 2 minutes + prep time (CSV)

---

## Step 4: Assign PACEs to Students

Students need PACE assignments before you can set daily goals. Zion includes the complete ACE PACE catalog (144 PACEs, 1001-1144).

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Student Detail">
    Click any student from the Students list to view their detail page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Assign PACE">
    In the "Current PACEs" section, click "Assign PACE" or "Manage PACEs".
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select PACEs">
    A modal opens with the PACE catalog. Select 4-5 PACEs across different subjects for the student:
    - Example: Math 1005, English 1015, Science 1025, Social Studies 1035
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Assignments">
    Click "Save" and the PACEs will appear in the student&apos;s profile.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Repeat for All Students">
    Repeat this process for all students. Each student can have individualized PACE assignments based on their progression.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
**Time Saver:** Most students in the same grade level will have similar PACE assignments. Assign PACEs to one student, then use their assignments as a reference for others.
</Tip>

**Time Required:** 5-10 minutes for 20-30 students

---

## Step 5: Set Your First Daily Goals

You&apos;re ready to use Zion&apos;s Goal Check feature! Let&apos;s set today&apos;s goals.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Goal Check">
    Click "Goal Check" in the sidebar, then click "Set Goals".
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Today's Date">
    The date picker defaults to today. Confirm the date is correct.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Goal Pages">
    For each student and PACE, enter:
    - Goal start page (e.g., 15)
    - Goal end page (e.g., 20)
    - Total pages auto-calculates (6 pages in this example)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Use Quick Templates (Optional)">
    Click "Copy Previous Day" to pre-fill goals from yesterday, or use standard templates:
    - Grades 1-4: 5 pages per day
    - Grades 5-8: 8 pages per day
    - Grades 9-12: 10 pages per day
  </Step>

  <Step title="Goals Auto-Save">
    As you type, goals auto-save. No "Submit All" button needed. Navigate away when done.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Time Required:** 5 minutes for 20 students (vs. hours of manual compilation!)

---

## Step 6: View Your Dashboard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Dashboard">
    Click "Dashboard" in the sidebar to see your school&apos;s real-time status.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore Dashboard Sections">
    - **Today&apos;s Snapshot:** Total students, completion %, at-risk count
    - **Weekly Trends:** 7-day completion rate chart
    - **Centre Performance:** Which centres are performing well
    - **Quick Actions:** Shortcuts to Goal Check, Students, Reports
  </Step>

  <Step title="Mark Progress (End of Day)">

  </Step>
</Steps>
